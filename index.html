Who We Are

About the Bulldog Dads

St. Isidore Bulldog Dads seeks to create and organically grow a multi-faceted platform to encourage and leverage past, current, and future SIS fathers' time, talent, and creativity, while fostering support of all types for St. Isidore School and its community members.
